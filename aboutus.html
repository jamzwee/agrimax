About Us

“ Food is the mother of all sustainability challenges”

Janet Ranganathan
VP for science and research, World Resources Institute

With growing populations, increased demand for bio-fuels, climate change, pollution, increasing water scarcity and loss of agricultural land to development, the world has to grow more with less. Agrimax was set up in 2014 to meet this challenge by developing products to reduce crop output gaps in a safe, sustainable manner. Our products are trademarked under DK-20 and can be found globally.
