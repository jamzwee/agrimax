Our Products

Better Quality, Higher Yield 

The world today is continually looking for solutions that can increase crop yields for food and bioenergy. The agricultural sector faces the challenge of devising and adopting more efficient and effective production methods to meet the requirements of sustenance, the significant bioenergy demand of the future and the issue of shrinking rural labor force. Additionally, in the coming decades, climate change and water security may bring further risks and unpredictability to crop harvests.

Agrimax’s range of products under the brand DK-20 is developed to increase quality and quantity of crops. DK-20 is environmentally non-threatening and capable of maximizing available arable land to increase crop yields for food, bioenergy and ornamental crops. The application of DK-20 to crops involves low technology and minimal additional resources for growers and can easily be implemented in conjunction with existing farming practices.

The successful field trials to date provide a strong indication that DK-20 works to modulate responses across a wide range of crops, independent of geographical locations. For farm use, DK-20 comes in pack sizes of 1L, 5L and 25L. Before use, the product is diluted according to recommended concentrations specific to crop type and application methods.

For home and garden use, convenient spray bottles in the form of ready-to-use are available. Applied according to recommended instructions, home users get to enjoy vibrant and healthier-looking plants, radiant flowers and lustrous foliage and lawn!

DK-20 Biostimulant

Using Agrimax’s proprietary fermentation process, our native soil bacteria in culture produce a complex composite of by-products. The result is a powerful biostimulant that acts as physiological triggers for plant growth and development. DK-20 biostimulant increases the plant’s metabolic efficiency, positively influencing its cell and tissue development, thereby maximising the plant’s innate potential. Applied at the right timing and stage of development, DK-20 biostimulant consistently elicit positive responses in crops, resulting in better quality produce and higher yield.

The plant growth enhancing properties of DK-20 biostimulant includes better root growth and greater root mass development, enhanced shoot and bud development, higher seed germination rates, better chloroplasts structures and higher photosynthetic output. These attributes generate stronger and healthier plants which are more efficient in nutrient and water uptake. Stronger stems and better foliar growth also improve the plant’s tolerance to abiotic stresses.   

DK-20 Liquid fertiliser Additive

A natural plant nutrition product which is easy-to-use and versatile, DK-20 liquid fertiliser additive improves the uptake and efficient use of fertilizers. It is suitable for all crops at all stages of development.

DK-20 liquid fertiliser additive is derived from the culture of Agrimax’s proprietary native soil bacteria and unique fermentation process. It is effective, safe and ecologically friendly. This additive has the ability to produce a positive, homogeneous response in crops and significantly increases yield across a variety of crops. With targeted application methods and recommended application rates, DK-20 liquid fertiliser additive works effectively on vegetables, fruit crops, grains and cereals, and ornamental plants. DK-20 liquid fertiliser additive improves crop quality and output for farmers.
 

DK-20 Trace Mineral Fertiliser

Derived from Agrimax’s proprietary fermentation process, DK-20 trace mineral fertiliser serves as an important supplement to the fertilisers used by farmers. DK-20 trace mineral fertiliser can be used together with existing crop protection products and fertiliser programs, ensuring the benefits from the latter are realised fully.

The unique quality of DK-20 trace mineral fertiliser is in the composition of micronutrient present; which plays an important role in balanced crop nutrition and plant growth. Effective across a wide variety of crops, DK-20 trace mineral fertiliser consistently improves yield through better developed root system, stronger stems, better leaf spread, higher chlorophyll content and increase in number of fruits per plant, higher fruit set and healthier fruit development.   

DK-20 Biofertiliser

DK-20 biofertiliser, manufactured using Agrimax’s proprietary fermentation process, delivers consistent results when applied to crops. This water based biofertiliser is eco-friendly, entirely natural and safe to use in conjunction with crop protection and fertiliser programs practised in different farms.

DK-20 biofertiliser improves plant cellular metabolism and development of plant vascular tissues. Application of DK-20 biofertiliser results in more efficient water and nutrient uptake and transportation in plants. DK-20 biofertiliser is effective across a variety of crops such as beverage and spice crops, cereals, legume crops, root/tuber crops, vegetables and melons and ornamental plants. Applied using the recommended method and at the right timing during the crop cycle, DK-20 biofertiliser induces better quality crop, higher yield and notable return on investment for the farmer.

With growing populations, increased demand for bio-fuels, climate change, pollution, increasing water scarcity and loss of agricultural land to development, the world has to grow more with less. Agrimax was set up in 2014 to meet this challenge by developing products to reduce crop output gaps in a safe, sustainable manner.
