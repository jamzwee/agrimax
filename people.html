Our People

Contributing to Agricultural Growth

Management Team

Mr Adam Tan
CEO

Mr Tan is the CEO of Agrimax. He has led the company since incorporation.

Mr Tan started his career in Merrill Lynch before leaving to manage Plasticscommerce Pte Ltd in 2000. Since 2000, the company has invested in and operated manufacturing companies in Europe and Russia, equipment distribution companies in Europe, an industrial automation company and a technical training school in Asia. Mr Tan is an Independent Non-Executive Director of Singapura Finance.

Mr Tan holds a Bachelor of Accountancy (Hons) from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and is a Chartered Accountant of Singapore (CA Singapore). 

Dr Kwa Siew Hwa
Chief Scientist and GM

Dr Kwa is the Chief Scientist and General Manager of Agrimax. She is responsible for all regulatory, research and development activities of the Company.

Prior to joining Agrimax, Dr Kwa was in-charge of global regulatory affairs in a local public listed company whose business is in crop and animal nutrition and protection. Dr. Kwa also has substantial experience in IP management, having held senior positions in Exploit Technologies Pte Ltd, a fully owned subsidiary of A*STAR, and the Industry and Technology Relations Office, NUS.

Dr Kwa holds a PhD degree in Botany from the NUS. Her area of research is in tissue culture and plant physiology and in terms of academic publications, Dr Kwa has ten international peer reviewed scientific papers to her credit. 

Board of Directors

Mr Ang Kong Hua 
Chairman of the Board

Mr Ang is Chairman of the Board of Agrimax Pte Ltd. Mr Ang started his career at the Economic Development Board. He then joined DBS Bank at its inception in 1968 and pioneered its investment banking division. From 1974, Mr Ang was CEO of NSL (formerly NatSteel) until he retired in 2003, and stayed as its Executive Director until 2010.

Mr Ang has helmed some of Singapore’s biggest companies, bringing years of experience spanning the manufacturing, services and financial sectors. His current appointments include chairmanship of Sembcorp Industries and Global Logistics. Mr Ang sits on the board of GIC Private Limited and chairs its Investment Board.

Mr Ang holds a Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Economics from the University of Hull. 

Mr Ming Z Mei
Non-Executive Director

Mr Mei is a Non-Executive Director of Agrimax. He is also Co-founder and CEO of GLP, a leading global investment manager specializing in logistics and related technology investments. Mr Mei has built GLP into one of the world's largest real estate and private equities fund manager, with over US$66 billion of assets under management. GLP continues to actively explore opportunities to grow the platform in new and existing markets.

Mr Mei is Chairman of Investment Committee of Eastern Bell Venture Capital and sits on various public and private boards. He is also the Investor and Board Member of Value Retail China, a company that specializes in the development and operation of luxury outlet shopping villages.

Mr Mei received his Bachelor of Science in Finance from the School of Business, Indiana University, USA. He also graduated from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University and holds a Master’s degree in Business Administration from the School of Business and Management, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Mr Quek Sim Pin
Non-Executive Director

Mr Quek is a Non-Executive Director of Agrimax. He is also currently serving as the Executive Chairman and a substantial shareholder of listed Acma Ltd.

After graduation, Mr Quek did a stint in general insurance underwriting, then joined Ford Motor Company (Singapore) as a Financial Analyst. He then joined Plessey plc as a Regional Finance Director for 8 years before leading a management buyout of the Plessey telecoms and computer operations in South East Asia. He injected these businesses into Acma Ltd in a reverse takeover in 1990 where he has been Executive Chairman to date.

Mr Quek graduated with a Bachelor of Business Administration degree (Hons) from the then University of Singapore (NUS) and is an Associate of the Chartered Insurance Institute.
