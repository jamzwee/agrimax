Our Brand

Transforming Fundamental Science Into Effective Products

Agrimax’s DK-20 range of products help farmers improve crop yield. DK-20 works through different mechanisms than fertilisers and act on the plant's vigour. DK-20 is complementary to crop nutrition and crop protection.
Scientific trials prove that DK-20’s unique composition delivers consistent and significant benefits to farmers.  

Why DK-20?

Farmers grow healthier and more resilient crops using DK-20, resulting in higher yield per crop cycle. DK-20 is the answer to farmers’ need for sustainable solution in order to stay competitive.

DK-20 is safe, non-toxic and ecologically friendly. DK-20 is all natural and does not contain any chemicals.  

What makes DK-20 different?

DK-20 is a complex composite of by-products from the culture of Agrimax’s proprietary native soil bacteria. DK-20 is sterile as the proprietary native soil bacteria is completely removed at the end of the culture.
Manufactured in state of the art bioreactors, product consistency is ensured through precision in process controls and stringent end process quality control protocols.
DK-20’s unique composition effectively enhances plant growth and development, thereby substantially increasing agricultural yield.

Providing optimal benefits

Agrimax works closely with farmers in devising DK-20’s application programs, ensuring optimal benefits to farmers and minimal disruption to existing farm practices. DK-20’s recommended application timings and rates are scientifically determined to enhance the different growth stages of specific crops.

How is DK-20 applied?

DK-20 can be used across different types of farming practices, including organic farming. DK-20 can be used as a seed soak, foliar spray, root dip, and/or root drench, depending on the variety of crops grown. A combination of two different application methods can be used for some crops.

Benefits of DK-20

Scientific trials across different geographical locations showed that DK-20 improves crop quality and seed germination rate. In addition, DK-20 also enhances efficiency of nutrient uptake and improves plant growth and development. DK-20 increases yield across a variety of crops – both food and ornamentals.

DK-20 also demonstrates the ability to extend shelf life of post-harvested crops such as vegetables, fruits and flowers.  

With growing populations, increased demand for bio-fuels, climate change, pollution, increasing water scarcity and loss of agricultural land to development, the world has to grow more with less. Agrimax’s vision is to meet this challenge by developing products to reduce crop output gaps in a safe, sustainable manner. Agrimax’s range of products is trademarked under the brand DK-20.
